gammawaves

distorted, swirling, fuzz-washed shoegaze set to dreamy, psychedelic visual art

Recording | Production | Video

gammawaves is an ongoing audiovisual art project combining my passion for soaring, atmospheric guitars and hazy, abstract video. Custom tunings and a chain of effects pedals produce a torrent of noise to accompany dreamlike visuals.

From my youthful interest in trance music through my ongoing obsession with shoegaze, I find myself drawn to the rush of euphoric, uplifting sounds. I create short, looping films to communicate this feeling visually. Find out more about gammawaves here.


rams

Unashamedly demonstrating my uber-cool, minimalist design credentials with an app

UI/UX | React | HTML.CSS.JS

Rams is a playful project built as a single-page application which can be bookmarked on mobile for native-like app functionality. A clean frontend delivers thoughts and ideas from the greatest industrial designer of all time.

There has been no bigger influence on my own aesthetic and design sensibilities than Dieter Rams, and I continue to reference his principles for good design daily. What better companion to a user-empathy and ideation session. Try out the application here.
