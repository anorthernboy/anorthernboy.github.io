goodgiving

Leading new product development in search of market position and revenue generating opportunities

User Research | MVP | UI/UX Design

GoodGiving aims to address an emergent request from client interviews - the consolidation of digital fundraising tools within one platform for financial reporting. As Product Manager and Designer I guided the product through design thinking ideation and testing before beta launch.

Functionality was defined and prioritised through competitive market analysis and I shared iterative prototypes with users before moving features into development. The product is optimised for AB testing as we aim to build donor conversion insights for charities.


city giving day

Innovating for good to enable participatory fundraising in unprecedented circumstances

Adobe XD | HTML.CSS.JS | UI/UX Design

The Lord Mayor's Appeal City Giving Day sees 400+ corporates host challenge-based fundraising initiatives in their offices. GoodBox proposed a digital solution for City Giving Day 2020 and I joined the project as Frontend Developer and Product Designer.

As well as colleague nominations, the application features a charity calculator which displays donation impact in real time - creating instant positive reinforcement which has been shown through GoodBox's hardware devices to increase donor conversion and donation value.
